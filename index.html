About Colby

"Real estate isn't just about properties — it's about finding the place where your life comes into focus."

Born and raised along California's sun-drenched coast, Colby Gray brings an intimate understanding of what it means to live well by the water. With a background in architecture and a passion for design, he approaches every transaction with a curator's eye — finding not just a house, but a home that reflects who you are.

As a top-producing agent with Coastal Premier Properties, Colby has guided clients through some of California's most coveted communities — from Malibu and Santa Barbara to La Jolla and Laguna Beach. His reputation is built on discretion, expert negotiation, and an unwavering commitment to exceeding expectations.
